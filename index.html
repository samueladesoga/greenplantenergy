Who We Are

Your Trusted Energy Partner

Green Plant Energy Oil and Gas Limited was founded with a simple mission: to make reliable diesel (AGO) supply accessible to every organisation and individual across Lagos, Nigeria — without delays, compromise, or uncertainty.

Based in Victoria Island, Lagos, we operate a modern fleet of fuel tankers and work with certified depot partners to ensure the AGO we deliver meets the highest quality standards. From Lekki to Ikorodu, Ikeja to Badagry, we cover every corner of Lagos State. Whether you need a one-off supply or a recurring fuel contract, we tailor our service to your schedule and budget.

  • Licensed and certified by the relevant regulatory authorities
  • Dedicated account managers for every client
  • Competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden charges
  • Flexible payment terms for verified corporate clients
